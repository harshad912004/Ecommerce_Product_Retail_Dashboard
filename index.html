Project Overview

Objective: To create an interactive Excel dashboard providing a unified view of sales data, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance and identify profitable product categories.

Problem: The company’s data was fragmented across regions and files, making it difficult to track category-level sales, profitability, and seasonal performance trends.

Approach:

  • Collected and cleaned transactional data using Power Query
  • Designed KPIs for revenue, profit margin, and product category performance
  • Developed interactive visualizations with Pivot Tables and slicers
  • Created automated refresh workflows to reduce manual reporting time

Key Insights:

  • Electronics generated 42% of total revenue
  • Accessories achieved the highest profit margin (36%)
  • Sales spiked during November–December (holiday season)
  • Top-performing regions: USA, Canada, and Germany

Recommendations:

  • Focus marketing spend on high-margin categories
  • Bundle low-performing items to boost turnover
  • Leverage seasonal demand through Q4 promotions

Outcome: Reduced manual reporting by 70% and improved visibility into product-level profitability, empowering the management team to make faster, data-driven decisions.
